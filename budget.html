Where Tagpuan fits

Why donations matter

On This Side of the World runs at the Irene Diamond Stage (294 seats). Five weeks, eight shows per week.

Possible tickets 11,760 294 seats × 40 performances
At 75% attendance 8,820 tickets sold
At $65 average ticket ~$573K gross ticket income
Production cost $1.025M gap before fundraising: ~$452K

Tickets help. They do not cover a full production. That is normal for nonprofit theatre.

A nonprofit theatre cannot live on tickets alone

Tagpuan’s annual budget mixes earned and contributed income, similar to peer companies.

  • 32% earned (tickets, merch, education, gala tickets)
  • 68% contributed (donors, grants, sponsors, Artist Circle)
