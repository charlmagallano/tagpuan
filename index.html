About

Tagpuan means meeting place. We put Filipino music, language, family, and history onstage with Off-Broadway rigor and tickets more people can afford.

We rent venues around the city. No owned building. Student tickets, community nights, and galas keep the seats full.

Backed by a Founding Artist Circle including Lea Salonga, Olivia Rodrigo, H.E.R., and Eva Noblezada.
